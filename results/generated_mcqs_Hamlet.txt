## MCQ 1
Question: According to the provided text, where can one find the Folger Shakespeare Library's digital texts?
A) www.folger.org
B) www.folgerdigitaltexts.org
C) www.shakespeare.org
D) www.digitalfolgertexts.org
Correct Answer: B) www.folgerdigitaltexts.org


## MCQ 2
Question:  What section precedes the "Textual Introduction" in the listed contents?
A) Synopsis

## MCQ 3
Question:  The Director of the Folger Shakespeare Library describes Shakespeare's language as:
A) Simple and easy to understand
B) Old and unusual, yet new
C) Dull and uninspiring
D) Modern and contemporary
Correct Answer: B) Old and unusual, yet new
