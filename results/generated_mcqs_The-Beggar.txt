## MCQ 1
Question: What profession did Lushkov falsely claim to be before admitting to being a former choir singer?
A) A doctor
B) A student and a village schoolmaster
C) A lawyer
D) A woodchopper
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What was the primary reason Lushkov initially lied about his profession?
A) To entertain himself
B) To get money
C) To gain employment
D) To avoid being arrested
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: Who did Lushkov credit with his eventual reformation and sobriety?
A) Skvortsov, the lawyer
B) Olga, the cook
C) The notary he worked for
D) The men who drove the furniture vans
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 4
Question: What was Skvortsov's initial reaction to Lushkov's lies?
A) Amusement
B) Indifference
